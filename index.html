About Me

You've made it this far and must be expecting some sort of explanation.

I'm from Toronto, Canada, and I've been desiging for 15 years. I started out in photography & photo editing moving to graphic design, then to web design, and now into product design. I'm mostly passionate about being able to pay rent and eat, but I also love crafting design & code solutions that help people do their jobs faster/save time and money/make their lives easier. One of my favourite things about my job is working on a product or feature from start to finish, then seeing it evolve over iterations. Working with an equally enthusiastic team inspires me to push myself harder to learn, improve my skills, and help others do the same.
When I'm not working, I like to draw graffiti-style art, read horror novels, and listen to heavy metal. I have some cats, a significant collection of band t-shirts, and an insatiable hunger for poutine.
